Why Join Us

Benefits That Reflect Our Values

We believe the people who care for our clients deserve to be cared for in return. Here's what we offer.

Competitive Compensation

We pay above-market rates because we believe talented, dedicated caregivers deserve to be valued financially. Regular performance reviews ensure your compensation grows with your experience.

Flexible Scheduling

Full-time, part-time, and PRN positions available across all shifts. We work with your schedule — not against it — because we know life outside of work matters.

Ongoing Training & CEUs

We invest in your professional growth through paid continuing education, in-house skills training, and access to certifications that advance your career trajectory.

Health & Wellness Benefits

Eligible full-time employees receive comprehensive health benefits including medical, dental, and vision coverage, because your health matters as much as our clients'.

Supportive Team Culture

You'll never work in isolation. Our supervisory nurses, care coordinators, and leadership team are available around the clock to provide guidance and support when you need it.

Clear Career Pathways

We promote from within and create personalized development plans for every team member. Many of our care coordinators and supervisors began their careers as front-line caregivers.
