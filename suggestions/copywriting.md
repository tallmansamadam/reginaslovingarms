# Copywriting suggestions

Before/after pairs. The current copy is warm and grammatically clean — these are tightening passes, not rewrites of voice.

## Voice principles to lean into

1. **Lead with the visitor's situation, not our service.** Most visitors are an adult child researching care for a parent. They're tired, worried, and skimming. Headlines should land on their situation before our solution.
2. **Concrete over abstract.** "24-hour care in your home" beats "comprehensive care solutions." Numbers, names, places earn trust.
3. **Use "you" and "your loved one."** Pages currently swing between "you" (good), "clients" (clinical), and "patients" (medical). Pick one stance and hold it. We recommend "you and your family" for marketing pages; reserve "clients/patients" for legal/privacy copy.
4. **Reduce "compassionate" use.** It appears 14+ times. Replace half with something more specific (e.g., "patient", "calm", "unhurried", "warm").

## Hero — index.html

**Current**
> **Caregiving with Love, Compassion, and Safety**
>
> In every moment, we prioritize love, compassion, and safety. Your trusted partner for quality in-home care throughout the Chicago area — bringing exceptional care directly to your door.

**Issues:** abstract values triplet, then immediately repeats the same triplet in the subhead. "Bringing exceptional care directly to your door" is jargon — care isn't a pizza.

**Proposed**
> **Help your parent stay home — with care your family can trust.**
>
> Caregivers, nurses, and companions across Chicagoland, available 24 hours a day. Insurance accepted. Free in-home assessment before any commitment.

Or, shorter (if the photo is doing the emotional work):
> **Home care your family can trust — across Chicagoland.**
>
> Free in-home assessment. Most insurance accepted. Care can start within 48 hours.

## Meta-description rewrites (SEO + click-through)

**index.html — current** (155 chars, good length)
> Regina's Loving Arms provides compassionate home health care services in the Chicago area including caregiver services, IDoA senior programs, companion care, and 24-hour home care.

**Proposed**
> Chicago-area home care from caregivers and nurses you can trust. Most insurance accepted. 24-hour care, IDoA programs, and free in-home assessments. Call 312-773-5553.

(Mentions price-objection ("most insurance accepted"), urgency-signal ("free in-home assessment"), and embeds the phone number — Google often shows the phone number directly in mobile SERPs.)

**services.html — current**
> Explore the full range of professional home health care services offered by Regina's Loving Arms — caregiver services, IDoA senior programs, companion care, and 24-hour home care.

**Proposed**
> Caregivers, companions, and 24-hour live-in care across Chicagoland. Personal-care help, dementia support, post-hospital recovery. See what fits your family.

## "Trust strip" microcopy

If you adopt the multi-stat stats bar (see `examples/index-stats-bar.html`), here's tested microcopy for each chip:

| Number | Label | Source / evidence |
|---|---|---|
| **24/7** | Care available · 365 days a year | Existing on-call |
| **48 hr** | From first call to first visit (typical) | Confirm with care team |
| **6+** | Major insurance plans accepted | Aetna, BCBS IL, Humana, Meridian, Molina, + LTC |
| **Free** | In-home assessment · no obligation | Already offered |

If "48 hr" isn't accurate, swap for "**100%**" matched-on-personality, "**0**" hidden fees, or a years-in-business number when confirmed.

## Words to find-and-replace site-wide

| Find | Why | Replace with |
|---|---|---|
| "compassionate" (in body copy, not headings) | Diluted from repetition | "warm", "patient", "unhurried", or cut |
| "trusted partner" | Marketing cliché | "the same caregivers visiting every week" or similar specifics |
| "seamlessly integrating" | Corporate-speak | "fits into your routine" |
| "elevate your quality of life" | Empty | Cut or replace with what changes |
| "exceptional care" | Self-praise | Show, don't tell |
| "comprehensive" (4+ uses) | Vague | "every level" or list what's covered |
