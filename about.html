Who We Are

Built on a Promise to Serve

Regina's Loving Arms Home Health Care, LLC was born from a deeply personal calling. After two decades of clinical nursing experience — and witnessing firsthand how the right care at the right moment can transform a life — our founder set out to build an agency rooted not in transactions, but in relationships.

We serve clients across the Chicago metropolitan area with a singular focus: to provide the kind of care we would want for our own family members. That means attentive listening, clinical excellence, and an unwavering commitment to the dignity and independence of every individual in our care.

Today, Regina's Loving Arms is a trusted partner for hundreds of families navigating the challenges of aging, chronic illness, recovery, and disability — and we carry that responsibility with humility and pride.

"I started this agency because I believed home care could be more than a service — it could be a lifeline. Every client we serve is someone's mother, father, grandparent, or child. We never forget that."
— Regina, Founder & CEO
What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These six principles shape every interaction, every care plan, and every decision we make.

Compassion

We approach every client with genuine empathy — listening deeply, responding thoughtfully, and always leading with kindness over protocol.

Integrity

We do what we say and say what we mean. Families trust us with their most vulnerable loved ones — that trust is sacred and non-negotiable.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest clinical and professional standards, continuously investing in training, technology, and best practices.

Dignity

Every person in our care is treated as a whole individual — with their own history, preferences, and inherent worth — not a diagnosis or a task list.

Community

We are deeply invested in the wellbeing of the Chicago metropolitan area, partnering with local organizations to strengthen the fabric of care available to all.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches in home health care — from care coordination technology to evidence-based treatment protocols — to deliver better outcomes for every client.

How We Work

Our Approach to Care

We believe great care is never one-size-fits-all. Every client relationship begins the same way: by truly listening.

1

Personalized Assessment

Every new client relationship begins with a comprehensive, no-obligation home assessment conducted by a registered nurse. We evaluate medical needs, home environment, personal preferences, and family dynamics to build a complete picture before a single care plan is written.

2

Tailored Care Planning

We develop individualized care plans in collaboration with clients, their families, and their physicians. Every plan is specific, measurable, and flexible — designed to evolve as needs change, and always centered on the client's own goals for health and independence.

3

Consistent, Monitored Care

Continuity matters. We carefully match caregivers to clients based on personality, skill set, and schedule, then actively monitor outcomes through regular supervisory visits, family communication, and clinical reviews. You are never left wondering how things are going.
