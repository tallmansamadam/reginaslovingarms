Our Story

Built on a
Promise to Serve

Regina's Loving Arms was born from a deeply personal calling. After years of clinical nursing experience — and witnessing firsthand how the right care at the right moment can transform a life — our founder set out to build an agency rooted not in transactions, but in relationships.

We serve clients across the Chicago metropolitan area with a singular focus: to provide the kind of care we would want for our own family members.

"I started this agency because I believed home care could be more than a service — it could be a lifeline. Every client we serve is someone's mother, father, grandparent, or child. We never forget that."
— Regina, Founder & CEO
