Our Story

Built on a
Promise to Serve

Regina's Loving Arms was born from a deeply personal calling. After managing the compassionate care for and hospice needs of his grandmother over the course of three years — our founder set out to build an agency rooted not in transactions, but in relationships.

We serve clients across the Chicago metropolitan area with a singular focus: to provide the kind of care we would want for our own family members. That means attentive listening, clinical excellence, and an unwavering commitment to the dignity and independence of every individual in our care.

"I started this agency because I believed home care could be more than a service — it could be a lifeline. Every client we serve is someone's mother, father, grandparent, or child. We never forget that."
— David, Founder & Owner
